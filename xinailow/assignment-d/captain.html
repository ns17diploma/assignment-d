Captain America: The First Avenger
In New York City, Steve Rogers is rejected for World War II military recruitment because of various health and physical
problems. While attending an exhibition of future technologies with his friend, Sgt. James "Bucky" Barnes, Rogers again
attempts to enlist. Overhearing Rogers' conversation with Barnes about wanting to help in the war, Dr. Abraham Erskine
allows Rogers to enlist. He is recruited into the Strategic Scientific Reserve as part of a "super-soldier" experiment
under Erskine, Col. Chester Phillips, and British agent Peggy Carter. Phillips is unconvinced by Erskine's claims that
Rogers is the right person for the procedure but relents after seeing Rogers commit an act of self-sacrificing bravery.
The night before the treatment, Erskine reveals to Rogers that Schmidt underwent an imperfect version of the procedure
and suffered permanent side-effects.
