THE MAZE RUNNER

Thomas (Dylan O'Brien), a teenager, arrives in a glade at the center of a giant
labyrinth. Like the other youths dumped there before him, he has no memory of his previous life.
Thomas quickly becomes part of the group and soon after
demonstrates a unique perspective that scores him a promotion to Runner status --
those who patrol the always-changing maze to find an escape route.
Together with Teresa (Kaya Scodelario), the only female,
Thomas tries to convince his cohorts that he knows a way out.
